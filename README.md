# 🏭 Steel Wizard AI

### AI-Powered Reliability & Maintenance Intelligence Platform

**Tagline:** Diagnose • Predict • Prioritize • Prevent

Steel Wizard AI is a production-ready industrial maintenance decision support platform designed for steel manufacturing environments. The system combines predictive maintenance, reliability engineering, root cause analysis, spare intelligence, and AI-assisted troubleshooting into a single operational command center.

---

## 🚀 Overview

Steel manufacturing plants operate thousands of critical assets where unexpected failures can result in:

* Production losses
* Safety risks
* Increased maintenance costs
* Equipment downtime
* Supply chain disruptions

Steel Wizard AI transforms maintenance from a reactive process into a proactive, intelligence-driven workflow by leveraging asset data, maintenance history, alerts, and engineering knowledge.
