# 📌 Overview

Steel Wizard AI is an Industrial Maintenance Intelligence Platform designed to help steel manufacturing plants reduce downtime, improve reliability, optimize maintenance planning, and make data-driven operational decisions.

The platform combines:

* Predictive Maintenance
* Reliability Engineering
* Asset Health Monitoring
* AI-Powered Maintenance Assistance
* Spare Inventory Intelligence
* Alert Management
* Executive Reporting

into a single intelligent decision-support system.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Steel manufacturing plants operate hundreds of critical assets.

Unexpected failures can lead to:

* Millions in production losses
* Safety risks
* Unplanned downtime
* Emergency maintenance costs
* Supply chain disruptions
* Inventory shortages

Traditional maintenance approaches are reactive.

Steel Wizard AI transforms maintenance operations into a proactive and predictive process using AI-driven insights.

# ✨ Key Features

## 🤖 AI Maintenance Wizard

Interact using natural language.

Example questions:

* Why is HSM Finishing Stand F4 critical?
* Which asset is most likely to fail?
* Generate a maintenance plan for BF-03.
* What is reducing plant reliability?
* Which spare parts should be reordered?
* Summarize all critical alerts.

AI-generated outputs include:

* Root Cause Analysis
* Risk Assessment
* Recommended Actions
* Required Spare Parts
* Estimated Downtime
* Business Impact
* Maintenance Recommendations
